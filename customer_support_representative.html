Customer Support Representative

RAG chatbot for food delivery support.

This page simulates a Swiggy-style customer support representative. The floating toolbar opens a grounded support chatbot that can answer policy questions, check real sample order records from a SQLite backend, and hand off the conversation to a human support-agent queue.

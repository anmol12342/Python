Conversation Preview

My order is 30 minutes late and I still do not see movement.
I am sorry your order is delayed. I can help you check whether pickup is still pending or the rider is already on the way, and if the delay becomes significant support can review escalation or compensation options.
Can I cancel now?
Cancellation is usually possible only before preparation starts. If the restaurant has already started preparing the food or the rider has been assigned, cancellation may be limited or only a partial refund may apply.

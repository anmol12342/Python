The Story

Born Shéyaa Bin Abraham-Joseph, 21 Savage has risen to become one of the most influential figures in modern hip-hop. Emerging from the Atlanta underground scene, he gained global recognition with his raw lyricism and distinct monotone flow.

His journey from the streets of East Atlanta to becoming a Grammy-winning artist and a voice for immigrant rights is one of the most compelling narratives in the industry today.

With multiple platinum records and critically acclaimed albums, he continues to redefine the sound of trap music while expanding his artistic range.

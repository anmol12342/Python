The Journey

Jacques Bermon Webster II, known professionally as Travis Scott, is a sonic architect and cultural phenomenon. From the hazy depths of Houston to the cosmic heights of "Astroworld," he has redefined the boundaries of hip-hop through high-octane performance and psychedelic production.

Cactus Jack Records

Not just a label, but a creative collective. Founded by Scott in 2017, Cactus Jack has collaborated with some of the biggest global brands, merging music, fashion, and lifestyle into a singular, dark aesthetic.
